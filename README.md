# WP-PFManagement™

**The low-code platform for WordPress.** Model your processes, assets and services — entities, fields, forms, lists, permissions and business rules — without writing a line of code, and ship real applications (ITSM, CRM, asset/CMDB, service desk) inside your own WordPress install, with no external SaaS and your data never leaving your database.

WP-PFManagement is a ServiceNow-style low-code layer, native to WordPress. It gives you structured data with a per-entity form layout, a sidebar of apps and modules, row- and field-level permissions, and an event catalog (record created, changed, deleted…) that the WP-PFWorkflow™ engine consumes natively — so your data and your automation work together. The [WP-PFAgent](https://github.com/Project-Flash-Build/wp-pfagent) AI agent can design an entity, generate its form and wire its events from a one-line description.

WP-PFManagement is a **proprietary, per-customer-licensed WordPress plugin**. The standard build ships obfuscated and is refundable; an optional annual add-on delivers the clean PHP source. It is sold and licensed — not distributed here.
